About Community Forest Watch (Bunjil)

For Local Conservation Groups in Remote Forest Areas..

We wish to protect our land from illegal clearing. So we want to know about any disturbances as soon as possible so that we can respond effectively.

We don’t know how to find or read satellite images and can’t afford to pay someone. If a service could just email or text us when our land is disturbed, we could better protect our land.

Who is creating Bunjil Forest Watch?

This app was created by Chris Goodman with help from students at Swinburne.

The app runs on Google App Engine and relies on Google Earth Engine and Landsat images from USGS

This app was supported by Object Consulting Pty. Ltd.
